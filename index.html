The Vibe

No stage. No back row. Just the room and the voices.

You've probably walked past it — a bright yellow room on Lygon Street that most of Melbourne only knows in daylight. For one Saturday, we're keeping it open past closing, and filling it with sound.

One guitar. Voices that don't stay on a stage, because there isn't one — our singers move through the room, close enough that you feel the note before you hear it. Drinks in hand. Sun through the windows turning gold as the afternoon rolls on.

It's Old School R&B — live, loose and full-hearted. The kind of Saturday you don't tell people about until it's over, because you want it kept just a little bit yours.

You had to be there. So be there.
